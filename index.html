Means of struggle

In keeping with revolutionary principles, and considering internal and external conditions, we will continue to fight by all means until we achieve our goal. In order to achieve its goal, the FLN must accomplish two fundamental tasks at once: internal action, both in the political field and in the field of pure action, and external action to make the Algerian cause a reality in the whole world, with the support of all our natural allies.
